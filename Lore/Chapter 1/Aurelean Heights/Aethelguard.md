# Aethelgard: The Ground Capital

The sprawling surface metropolis of [[The High Table]], serving as the primary commercial gatehouse and trade terminal of the empire.

## The Economic Hub
While the elite aristocrats govern from the floating safety of [[Aurealis]], Aethelgard handles the physical wealth of the nation. It is a massive trading hub constructed directly on the surface floor, situated right beneath the shadow of the floating sky islands. All ground commerce, maritime imports, and caravan networks converge here.

## Architecture & Defense
Unlike the sky city, Aethelgard cannot rely on geographic height for safety. To protect their immense mercantile investments from land-based raiders like [[The Umbral Vanguards]], the city features:
* **The Sovereign Walls:** Massive, multi-layered defensive walls built entirely of reinforced, stark-white **Luminite**.
* **The Grand Markets:** A highly organized central district of white stone courtyards where foreign merchants, surface trades, and sky-bound goods are heavily taxed and exchanged.
