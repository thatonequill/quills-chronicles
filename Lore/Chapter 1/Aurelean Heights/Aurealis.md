# Aurealis: The Pristine City

The crown jewel of [[The High Table]], serving as the supreme capital and the center of global high society.

## The Sky City
Aurealis is situated on a cluster of massive floating islands high above the clouds within the **Aurelean Heights**. This geographic isolation keeps the ruling class completely separated from the chaos of the lower world, allowing them to conduct the empire's grandest business affairs in absolute safety. 

## Architecture & Grandeur
Built entirely from white **Luminite** and glistening gold accents, the city is a masterpiece of high-society architecture. Cascading waterfalls flow from the edges of the floating islands into the world below, and pristine towers house the grand counting houses, diplomatic embassies, and royal courts of the merchant lords.

## Historical Significance
Due to its position as the ultimate symbol of wealth, Aurealis became the primary target of the Vanguards' ambitions, leading directly to the events of [[The Historic Catastrophe]].
