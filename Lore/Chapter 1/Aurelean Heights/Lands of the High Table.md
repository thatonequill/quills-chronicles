# Lands of the High Table

The domains under the control of [[The High Table]] are bright, naturally rich, and designed to showcase the immense wealth of the empire.

## The Aurelean Heights (Sky Biome)
The crown jewel of the empire's territory. This is an ultra-rare sky biome characterized by a sprawling archipelago of massive floating islands suspended high above the clouds. The air is pristine, and the climate is eternally bright and serene. This geographic isolation gives the high-society elite absolute safety from the chaos of the lower world.

## The Sun Bathed Plains
A sprawling, golden biome that serves as the agricultural and commercial highway of the surface empire. These plains are constantly illuminated by bright sunlight, facilitating massive trade expeditions and heavily guarded cargo routes.

## The Gilded Canopy (Forest Biome)
An ethereal, bright yellow woodland that grows along the borders of their territory. The environment is highly poetic and radiant.

### Flora
* **Sun-Silk Birch:** A rare, majestic tree species native to the canopy. They feature smooth white bark and gold leaves that shimmer under the sunlight.

### Architectural Blocks
The High Table builds to project permanence and pristine luxury, utilizing highly refined materials for their castles and fortifications:
* **Luminite:** A smooth, stark-white stone derivative of diorite. It forms the foundation of all high-society architecture.
* **Gold Trim & Gem Inlays:** Structural walls are heavily accented with real gold leaf trim and inlaid with precious diamonds and amethysts to display the empire's limitless wealth.
