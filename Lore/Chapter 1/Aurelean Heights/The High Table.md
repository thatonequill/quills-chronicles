# The High Table

A wealthy, pristine, and aristocratic civilization of master diplomats who govern their empire with calculating precision and an absolute focus on economic growth.

## Culture & Diplomatic Philosophy
The High Table values prosperity above all else. They view the entire world not as territory to conquer by blood, but as a vast marketplace where every entity is a potential client and source of revenue. They are calm, collected, and inherently averse to unnecessary warfare. However, their polite diplomacy should not be mistaken for weakness, they are fiercely protective of their commercial networks and will ruthlessly eliminate any entity that threatens their economic bottom line.

## The High Table Crest
> "An emblem of absolute authority, unyielding wealth, and central power."

* **Visual Structure:** A perfectly symmetrical, isometric golden hexagon. The heavy, polished borders catch the light from all angles, symbolizing a faction that stands unshakeable, deeply rooted, and architecturally secure. 
* **The Central Motif:** Embedded at the heart of the golden frame is a flawless, diamond-cut cerisium heart. It represents the vital "lifeblood" of the realm's economy, as well as the exclusive, closely-guarded core of the elite ruling class.
* **Thematic Significance:** The warm, radiant gold and brilliant crimson tones broadcast immense prestige and governance. There are no sharp, aggressive edges here—only the geometric perfection of a faction that commands order, dictates laws, and sits comfortably at the top of the food chain.

## Commerce & Metallurgy: The Forge Path
Technology within High Table society is deeply intertwined with magic, status, and diplomacy. They grind down hoarded gold and emeralds into a refined "Opulent Dust," which is then used to forge their elite **Aristocrat Steel armor**. Equipment forged along this path is designed to project power and leverage, granting the coveted *Hero of the Village* status to its wielders. This specialized metallurgical tree ultimately leads to the legendary Tier 7 Cerisian Gear.

## Foreign Relations
* **[[The Umbral Vanguards]]:** Targets for total elimination. Originally tolerated as a minor, manageable nuisance to surface trade, they are now marked for systematic destruction following the events of the [[Annihilation of a Civilization]].
* **[[Lost Civilization]]:** One of the High Table's most lucrative historical trading partners. The High Table is currently mobilizing to salvage their [[Lost Knowledge]].
