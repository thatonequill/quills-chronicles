# High Table Infrastructure & Minor Settlements

The regional network of smaller fortified outposts that secure the trade routes across [[The High Table]]'s territory.

## The Prefectures
Scattered strategically across the **Sun Bathed Plains**  are numerous Prefectures, small, heavily fortified, walled towns. 

Rather than centers of high culture, these settlements exist purely to support the High Table's economic machine:
* **Banking Chambers:** They act as secure regional banking centers and heavily guarded storage vaults for hoarding gold and emerald dust collected from the surrounding lands.
* **Garrison Keeps:** Each prefecture houses a permanent garrison of legions tasked with patrolling the highways and keeping the cargo routes completely clear of pirate scouts.
* **Uniform Design:** To project the absolute authority and pristine luxury of the crown, every prefecture is built to strict architectural standards, featuring central marketplaces and defensive structures made of white **Luminite**.
