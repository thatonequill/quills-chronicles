# Vanguard Infrastructure & Minor Settlements

[[The Umbral Vanguards]] reject centralized governance, spreading their forces across hidden networks throughout the dark biomes.

## Decentralized Encampments
These are small, highly mobile scouting and military camps hidden across various border biomes. Built from temporary canvas tents, crude palisades, and portable forge blocks, these camps allow Vanguard raiding parties to stage surprise assaults on [[The High Table]] shipping routes before vanishing back into the wilderness.

## Hidden Townships
Deep within the **Twilight Canopy**, buried beneath the thick, interlocking branches of the **Dusk Redwoods**, lie the larger Vanguard villages. 
* **Camouflage:** These townships are completely invisible from the sky, entirely protected from the aerial scouts of [[The High Table]].
* **Construction:** Houses and communal longhalls are constructed from the purple heartwood of the redwoods, reinforced with crude iron framing and volcanic bricks. These settlements serve as the residential and agricultural heart of the pirate faction, housing the families, builders, and suppliers who keep the armada armed.
