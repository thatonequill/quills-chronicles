# Lands of the Vanguards

The territories claimed by [[The Umbral Vanguards]] are treacherous, shrouded in darkness, and naturally hostile to outsiders.

## The Twilight Canopy (Forest Biome)
The primary hunting and staging grounds for the faction are the **Dusk Redwood forests**. These are massive, foggy woodlands characterized by giant, towering conifers that aggressively block out the sun. The forest floor exists in a state of perpetual dim light and thick fog, allowing Vanguard scouts to move completely unseen.

### Flora
* **Dusk Redwoods:** Colossal trees featuring deep purple heartwood and feathery needles that pierce the heavy mountain mists. 

## The Volcanic Peaks
Surrounding the forests are the jagged ridges of the **Eclipse Highlands**. The environment is a brutal contrast of black rock and freezing mountain air.

### Geological Blocks
* **Nyxite:** A dark gray, volcanic stone similar to obsidian or raw andesite. It is incredibly dense, heat-blasted, and forms the bedrock of all Vanguard defensive engineering.
* **Crude Mineral Veins:** Exposed, glowing lines of unrefined minerals cutting through the dark mountain stone.
