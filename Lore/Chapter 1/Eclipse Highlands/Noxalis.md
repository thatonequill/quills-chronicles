# Noxalis: The Veiled Citadel

The supreme stronghold of [[The Umbral Vanguards]], serving as the central seat of their military command and heavy industry.

## Architecture & Defense
Carved directly into the heart of the **Eclipse Highlands**, Noxalis is a brutalist fortress built entirely from dark **Nyxite** stone. The citadel is strategically positioned within a jagged, rugged mountain range where the black volcanic peaks are capped with blinding white snow. 

The architecture is entirely utilitarian, designed to withstand both heavy sieges and the freezing elements:
* **Bunker Engineering:** Subterranean quarters and command chambers are sealed behind heavy iron-banded doors.
* **Slatted Hatching:** Defensive battlements utilize heavy iron slatted bunker hatches to rain projectiles down on invaders while protecting the defenders.
* **The Core Forges:** The lowest levels of the citadel house the grand, soot-stained industrial smelting yards where the faction's heavy combat alloys are continuously hammered out.
