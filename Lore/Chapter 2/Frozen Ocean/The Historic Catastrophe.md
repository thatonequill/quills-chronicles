# The Grand Naval Defeat

A massive, tragic event in the military history of [[The Umbral Vanguards]], serving as a permanent monument to their failed conquest.

## The Failed Siege
In the past, the Vanguards launched a massive armada of raiding ships into the open waters, intending to scale the skies and lay siege to the floating city of [[Aurealis]]. The campaign was a catastrophic failure, resulting in the total immobilization and destruction of the Vanguard fleet.

## The Ship Graveyard
The remnants of the armada remain trapped directly beneath the **Aurelean Heights**. This less-rare, frozen ocean biome serves as a massive ship-graveyard where the ruined warships are frozen permanently in place within the ice sheets.
