# Civilization from the Depth

An ancient oceanic empire that existed prior to the outbreak of the current surface war.

## History & Fate
* **Commercial Legacy:** The civilization was long established as one of [[The High Table]]'s most lucrative trading partners, supplying them with rare deep-sea materials and commerce. 
* **The Purge:** The empire was completely wiped out and destroyed during a brutal maritime expansion campaign launched by the raiding fleets of [[The Umbral Vanguards]].

* `[WIP]` The names of specific cities, historical figures, and regional biomes are currently unmapped.
