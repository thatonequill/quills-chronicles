# Lost Knowledge

The scattered mystical and metallurgical secrets left behind in the ruins of the [[Lost Civilization]]. This data is the primary catalyst for the current total war between the surviving surface factions.

## The Forge Paths & Advanced Metallurgy
Technology is dictated by class, and accessing this knowledge is required to master high-tier crafting infrastructure:

* **The Dusting Table:** A specialized workstation used to grind hoarded gems (like gold and emeralds) into reactive dusts, allowing the mastery of advanced alloys.
* **The High Table Path:** Leads to magic and diplomacy via *Aristocrat's Alloy* (which grants the *Hero of the Village* effect).
* **The Vanguard Path:** Leads to heavy, brutal combat gear via *Netherite Steel*.
* **The Convergence:** Both opposing metallurgical paths ultimately converge at the highest tier to create the legendary **Tier 7 Cerisian Gear** (utilizing the ultra-rare cherry-red *Cerisium* gemstone).
