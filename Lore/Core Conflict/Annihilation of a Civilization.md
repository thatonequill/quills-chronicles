# The Annihilation of the Deep

The spark that ignited total war across the world was a campaign of absolute devastation executed by the raiding fleets of the mountain pirates.

## The Fallen Empire
Driven by an insatiable hunger for new plundering grounds and resources, [[The Umbral Vanguards]] pushed their frontiers beyond the coastlines. They launched an unprecedented assault into the open waters, targeting and completely destroying the [[Lost Civilization]]. The onslaught was absolute; it was not a war of conquest or subjugation, but a swift, brutal purging that left an ancient oceanic empire entirely in ruins.

## The Commercial Collapse
The destruction of the deep citizens was met with a cold, calculated calm within the sky spires of [[The High Table]]. As master diplomats and merchants, they knew better than to panic. Instead, they quietly assessed the damage to their bottom line: the aquatic empire had long been established as one of the High Table’s most lucrative trading partners. The sudden collapse of this oceanic trade network instantly choked off the flow of rare materials, exotic commerce, and invaluable revenue that the merchant aristocrats relied upon to maintain their lavish society.

## The Hunt for the Legacy
The High Table mobilized their legions for total war, shifting from a posture of defensive border protection to aggressive mobilization. Their primary objective is not vengeance, but asset acquisition. 

The fallen deep dwellers possessed highly coveted secrets regarding advanced metallurgy and mysticism. With that civilization erased, those secrets are now scattered through the dark waters as [[Lost Knowledge]]. The High Table has accepted open warfare to secure this knowledge for themselves, determined to salvage the metallurgical secrets to protect their global economic dominance before the Vanguards can dig them up and weaponize them.
